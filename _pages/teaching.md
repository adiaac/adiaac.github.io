## PBHLTH 224W: Organizational Behavior and Management in Health Care
- University of California, Berkeley
- Instructor: Hector P. Rodriguez
- Graduate Student Instructor (Fall 2025)

*Winner, 2026 Outstanding Graduate Student Instructor Award*

Introduction to health administration, focusing on theories of management, organizations, and environments as they relate to the administration of health services. Cases, simulation, and structured experiences are used to tie theory to practice. Course includes MPH and MBA students.

## PHP 1680U: Intersectionality and Health Disparities
- Brown University
- Instructor: Jennifer Nazareno
- Graduate Teaching Assistant (Fall 2018)

This course provides a broad overview of health disparities in the U.S., specifically, examining them through intersecting structural and social factors. Undergraduate and master's level course.

## PHP 1070: The Burden of Disease in Developing Countries
- Brown University
- Instructor: Stephen McGarvey
- Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (Fall 2016); Head Teaching Assistant (Fall 2017)

This is an advanced introduction to global public health, defining and critically examining key topics and concepts through an interdisciplinary lens. Undergraduate and master's level course.
